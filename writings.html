I just ate it cold

Genre: Poetry

I found myself here
In line, waiting
The gold tinted lights
Hanging off the stained, chipped wall
The man in front of me, towering over
I can't see the menu
But I already know what I want
It still doesn't help
My words come out, breaking
Chipped
As if I'm trying too hard
The lady was kind, she knew
-----
So I wondered
I just ate it later
Cold.
